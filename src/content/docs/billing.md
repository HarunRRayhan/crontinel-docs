## Plans overview

| | Free | Pro | Team |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Apps** | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| **Data retention** | 7 days | 90 days | 365 days |
| **Team members** | — | 3 | Unlimited |
| **Alert channels** | — | Slack, email, webhook | All channels |
| **Price** | Free | $19/mo or $182/yr | $49/mo or $470/yr |

## Free plan

The free plan is designed for single-developer projects and local evaluation. It includes:

- 1 app
- 7-day data retention
- Basic cron monitoring (exit code, duration)
- Queue depth monitoring

No credit card required.

## Upgrading

Go to **Settings → Billing** in your dashboard and choose a plan. Upgrades take effect immediately with a prorated charge for the remainder of your billing cycle.

## Downgrading

Downgrades apply at the **end of your current billing period** — you keep full access until then. No prorated refund is issued for the unused portion.

## Canceling

Cancel from **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**. Your subscription stays active until the end of the current billing period. After that, your account moves to the free plan.

## Data after downgrading or canceling

Your data is retained according to your plan's retention period. When you downgrade or cancel, any data beyond the new plan's limit is removed immediately.

## Payment

All payments are processed through Stripe. Crontinel never stores your card details.

Invoices appear in **Settings → Billing → Invoice History** after each payment.
